JOEL.

Nell! Why, ain't she comin' here just a-purpose to get you? Ain't there reason enough, Lord knows? Ain't you made up your mind to light out home anyhow?

ANDREW.

Yes. That's just what she'll never forgive me for. In her heart she'll never think of me the same. For she knows as well as I what pledge I'll be breaking--what sacred pledge.

JOEL

What you mean?

ANDREW.

No matter, no matter; this is gush.

[_He returns to the fire and begins to fumble over the contents of his knapsack._ JOEL _watches him idly_.]

JOEL

One of _her_ curls?

ANDREW.
